About Me

Hello! I'm Tejas

I'm a lifestyle and portrait photographer based in Sangli. I specialize in capturing real, raw, and timeless moments using natural light and creative composition. Whether it's a candid wedding shoot or a studio portrait, my goal is to create memories that last forever.

My Journey

I started photography as a hobby during college, and it quickly turned into a passion. Over the past 6 years, I’ve photographed more than 200 happy clients and collaborated with top brands, influencers, and families.

What I Offer

I offer photo shoots for weddings, engagements, family portraits, baby showers, branding, and more. I work across India and also travel for destination shoots.

Why I Do It

For me, photography is storytelling. I believe every photo should reflect your personality and emotion. That’s why I focus on building a connection with each client and making them feel confident in front of the camera.

Outside the Lens

When I'm not behind the camera, I enjoy hiking, cooking, and editing in cozy cafés. I love music and always bring good vibes to my sessions!
